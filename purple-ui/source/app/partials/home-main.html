Online Standup made easy

We've all been taught that it's important to have daily standup meetings. These are meant to encourage information flow between teams, but the problem is that they're actually structured to restrict information flow. And with distributed teams, this becomes an even bigger problem.
Stausnap sends you daily reminders to fill in your status updates for the day. Just fill 3 items - What you have worked on? What are you working on? and Are there any blockers.

FAQ

Q.How do I get started?

1) Sign up.
2) Invite your team members to join Statusnap.
3) Create the projects that your organization/team is working on. Assign members to these projects.
4) Decide when you want to have your daily status update.
Thats it. Now don't bother logging in everyday. Status snap will send you reminders everyday to fill your status update.

Q.What can I track?

1) Daily Progress of your team
2) Track work done against time
3) Analyze Project/User time metrics

Q.What's an Enterprise version?

You can either Signup with the cloud verion of Statusnap or you can have Statusnap on a dedicted machine either on the cloud or within your enterprise. Statusnap will help you setup the application and handhold you till things get rolling.

Q.What if I have more than 5 users?
