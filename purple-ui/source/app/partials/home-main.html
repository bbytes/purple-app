Online Standup Made Easy

Status Updates - Anywhere, Anytime.

It is important to have daily standup meetings. These are meant to encourage information flow between teams; but the problem is that they are actually structured to restrict information flow. And with distributed teams, this becomes an even bigger problem. Statusnap sends you daily reminders to fill in your status updates for the day. Just fill 3 fields - What you have worked on?, What are you working on?, and Are there any blockers?

Status update in less than a minute! Get to see updates from the rest of the team too!

FAQ

Q.How do I get started?

1) Sign up.
2) Invite your team members to join Statusnap.
3) Create projects that your organization or team is working on. Assign members to these projects.
4) Decide the time when you want to have your daily status update.
That's it. Now don't bother logging in everyday. Statusnap will send you reminders everyday to fill your status update.

Q.What can I track?

1) Daily Progress of your team.
2) Track work done against time.
3) Analyze Project and User time metrics.

Q.What's an Enterprise version?

You can either signup with the cloud verion of Statusnap or you can have Statusnap on a dedicted machine either on the cloud or within your enterprise. Statusnap will help you setup the application and handhold you till things get rolling.

Q.What if I have more than 5 users?

Currently, Statusnap is in the beta version and we are not charging our users. Soon you would be able to get your whole organization/team onboard Statusnap. Trust us, it's not going to be expensive!

Online standup and status tracking has never been this easy. Keep track of your team's progress on a daily basis and stay informed. Statusnap also provides stakeholders with user & project performance metrics, thus, helping you keep track of project progress.
